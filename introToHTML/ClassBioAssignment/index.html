Hello! My name is Arieh-Lev Zitron, Lev for short, I'm a 20 year old canadian game development student, currently studying at the Vancouver Film School (VFS). I enjoy playing all sorts of games: fast paced boomer shooters - think the new DOOM games; sandboxes like Minecraft, Terraria, and most recently No Man's Sky; and most importantly to me, strategy games. I grew up on simple strategy games that emphasized emergent complexity: Chess, Battle for Wesnoth, Advance Wars, even Civilization, all of which. My have and continue to inspire my game design sensibilities. My dream is to begin my own studio with some close friends, and really focus all our efforts and funding into creating the games we want to make, not the games the market needs. I think that's how you create the highest quality art.
